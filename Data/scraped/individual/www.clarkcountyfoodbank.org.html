Alleviating Hunger and its Root Causes in Clark County, Washington

Clark County Food Bank is a regional food bank that distributes 6.6 million pounds of food and 5.5 million meals a year.
We support 43 partners at 130 distribution sites, along with Food Bank led programs to serve the food insecure in our community.
With 65,000 hungry individuals in Clark County, the need is great, but we are here to help.
