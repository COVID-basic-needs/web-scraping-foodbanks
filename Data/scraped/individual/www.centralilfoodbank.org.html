Give Help

Central Illinois Foodbank depends on generous donors to continue operation. Donations ensure that we may cover the operational and transportation costs associated with acquiring and distributing the millions of pounds of food that pass through our facility each year. Your donation allows us to continue delivering nutritious food to those in our communities who need it most. We appreciate donations of any amount.
