Our Mission

The mission of Harvest Hope Food Bank is to provide for the needs of hungry people by gathering and sharing quality food with dignity, compassion, and education.

Host A Food Drive

Harvest Hope Food Bank works with thousands of generous South Carolina residents, schools, churches, businesses, and organizations each year through organized food drives. Food Drives are one of the biggest ways that you can make a difference in the community around you and help us put an end to hunger. We offer opportunities for both virtual and traditional food drives, which both help to keep our shelves stocked. Find out how you can get involved today!
