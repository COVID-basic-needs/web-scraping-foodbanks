At the Greater Baton Rouge Food Bank, there is no higher priority than the safety of our volunteers, staff and the community we serve.

Our Mission

Our mission is to feed the hungry in Baton Rouge and the surrounding parishes by providing food and educational outreach through faith-based and other community partners. With community support, we have served the hungry in our 11 parish service area for more than 30 years.

Empty Bowls – April 4, 2020

Guests purchase a $20.00 ticket to attend the event. This ticket allows them to choose an original, handmade, ceramic bowl to use for their meal. After the meal, guests are allowed to keep the bowl as a reminder of all the empty bowls in the world. One hundred percent of each meal’s proceeds are devoted to fighting hunger through the Greater Baton Rouge Food Bank.
