Our mission statement: Feeding the needy by – “Working in partnership with local non-profit agencies to eradicate hunger in Marion County.”

First Step Food Bank, Inc. runs an efficient and effective organization as shown by distributing 2,744,450 pounds of food products in FY 2013-2014 with a staff of 1-full-time employee, 4-part-time employees, and a volunteer Board of Directors.  (A full-time Executive Director, a part-time Office Manager, a part-time Development Director/Fundraising Coordinator, and 2-part-time truck drivers.)

The need for charitable gifts of food has continued as shown by the USDA’s 2013 food insecurity rate of 16.8% for the Marion County.  First Step Food Bank addresses this need issue by:

1) Receiving food products via: a Feeding America contractual relationship with Second Harvest Food Bank of Central Florida as a Partner Distributing Organization (PDO); purchases using monetary donations; and food donations from local grocers, organizations, and individuals.  AND

2) Distributing received food products to its approved, partner agencies as soon as possible.  First Step Food Bank has 44 partner agencies which operate 38 emergency food pantries, seven special needs residential facilities and three soup kitchens that distribute food products and prepared meals to individuals and families needing assistance in Marion County, FL.
