ABOUT US

  1. 1
  2. 2
  3. 3

It is hard for many of us to imagine not knowing where our next meal is going to come from, but hunger is a reality that one in six Oklahomans deal with every day. Our state consistently ranks in the top states in the nation in the number of people who struggle with hunger.

Located in Tulsa, the Community Food Bank of Eastern Oklahoma distributes food and other grocery items to 350 Partner Agencies in eastern Oklahoma, including food pantries, emergency shelters, soup kitchens, senior citizen centers and after-school programs. In Fiscal Year 2019, the Food Bank distributed more than 28.9 million pounds of food throughout the 24 counties of eastern Oklahoma.
