Armstrong County Community Action Agency

 

Armstrong County Community Action Agency is a non-profit organization incorporated in March 1965 as a community-based grassroots organization serving the low-income and disadvantaged.  Over the years, Community Action has established itself as one of the single largest providers of comprehensive human services in this community.

 
 

Today, it assists individuals with such concerns as nutrition, housing, transportation, education, job training, job placement and self sufficiency
