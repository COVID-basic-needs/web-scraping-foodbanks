Newsletter

The North Side Pantry Project, a new direct services program of the Toledo Northwestern Ohio Food Bank located in the Caldwell Community Center, is an initiative to combat hunger in the Toledo area food desert by providing a new source of healthy foods for the residents of north Toledo. This area maintains a poverty rate of more than 50% and has been underserved by regional food distribution for many years, further impacting the health of this community.
