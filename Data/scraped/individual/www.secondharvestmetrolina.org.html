Second Harvest Food Bank of Metrolina has always prioritized the health and safety of our community, volunteers and staff. Food safety has been a number one goal of the Food Bank’s since we started in 1981. Our 5th Annual Santa Express will run from December 3 - December 12. The event will provide holiday gifts of apples to each school along with items like toiletries, socks, school supplies and shoes to 15,000 high need elementary school students in 27 schools in 13 counties served by Second Harvest Food Bank of Metrolina. ....Follow The Santa Express Here »
