                Drive-Thru Meal Service

                Drive-Thru Meal Service
                During the extended break, March 16 - March 20, Alamo Heights ISD Child Nutrition Services is providing a drive-thru meal service for students who currently receive free or reduced-price meals.

                Meals (Breakfast and Lunch) can be picked up:
                Monday, March 16 from 10 am - 12 noon
                Tuesday, March 17 - Friday, March 20 from 8 - 10 am
                at Alamo Heights Junior School

                Families can arrive at the front of the school, driving through the Meal Pick-Up line, located at the north entrance of the cafeteria.

                Look for our friendly AHISD Cafeteria Staff who are happy to support our students!

                Questions?  Contact Richard Mallard, AHISD Child Nutrition Services, at rmallard@ahisd.net or by phone at (210) 832-5808 or (248) 633-4433

                (Updated Saturday, March 14, 2020)
