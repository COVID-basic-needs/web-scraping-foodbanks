About Our Food Bank in Texarkana, AR

Harvest Regional Food Bank has helped feed Northeast Texas and Southwest Arkansas families for over 25 years. We accept and distribute food that is served at local food pantries, soup kitchens, shelters, schools and other agencies that provide hunger relief. We have many opportunities available for those who want to help (through donations or volunteering), and for those who want to host a food drive or special event for the Food Bank.

Become a Food Bank Partner Agency

If you'd like to support the fight against hunger, Harvest Regional Food Bank would like to partner with you. We have teamed up with dozens of churches, shelters, schools and other organizations that want to provide food assistance to Texarkana and surrounding areas. Partner with us, and we'll all be one step closer to solving hunger here, and around the world. To learn more about becoming a food bank partner agency in Texarkana, call Harvest today.

Donate to or Volunteer at Our Food Bank

You can make a difference with your contribution. It's amazing how far a few hours or a few dollars can go. Helping feed children and families is a priceless opportunity. Donate or volunteer at our facility in Texarkana, AR today. Call us directly at (870) 774-1398 to discover all the opportunities you have to make a difference in the community.

Support the Fight Against Hunger with Harvest Regional Food Bank

Harvest Regional Food Bank is fighting hunger in Northeast Texas and Southwest Arkansas. For 26 years, we have been serving our 10-county area with food. Hundreds of thousands of individuals and families have found relief through our services, and we want to continue our work toward making our region one where people don't have to be hungry. But we can't do it alone. Learn more about our hunger relief services on this site, and if you would like to help out or donate, reach out to us to get involved. Because together, we can solve hunger.
