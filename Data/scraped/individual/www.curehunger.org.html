COVID-19 RESPONSE

Click Here for Updates

Second Harvest Food Bank of ECI, the region’s largest hunger-relief organization with a network of 96 agency partners and 35 schools in Blackford, Delaware, Grant, Henry, Jay, Madison, Randolph and Wabash counties, is carefully monitoring developments regarding COVID-19 (coronavirus).

Our goal, at this time, is to increase food availability to our communities in phases. As quickly as we coordinate new efforts, we will implement new procedures to get the food out. Please watch this space for continuing and up-to-date information.

Annual Volunteers 4,300+

At Second Harvest, we help feed hundreds of children, families and seniors every week. In order to do so, we rely on the generous support of individuals, corporations, and community partners.
