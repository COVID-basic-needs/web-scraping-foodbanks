Ending hunger in west central Wisconsin.

We believe no one should go hungry. In 2019, our food bank distributed roughly 7 million pounds of food to 200+ hunger-relief programs. Over 70,000 individuals living in our 14 county service area relied on food supplied by Feed My People.

Learn about our food bank programs

Feed My People Food Bank is an independent, nondenominational, 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization and a distribution outlet partner for Second Harvest Heartland, a member food bank of Feeding America.
