About ICCAP

Get to know us

ICCAP provides programs aimed at helping low-income families and individuals attain self sufficiency. Our programs provide new ways to solve household problems, manage emergencies, learn new living skills, and foster community involvement. We also examine and promote methods by which organizations and institutions can deal with the problems of rural and disadvantaged people.




Food Programs

ICCAP has an ongoing food distribution program in convenient locations throughout the county, operated entirely by volunteers.

Housing Programs

A number of housing services to help those struggling to pay their rent or mortgage.
