    Food Bank of Northwest Indiana's Mobile Market provides FREE fruits, vegetables, protein, dairy and other groceries to any Northwest Indiana resident in need of food assistance.   • Join the Fight Against Hunger
    Volunteers are critical to the Food Bank mission, and more than 1,400 people lend their time and talents every year to help fight hunger. Group and individual opportunities exist to help provide food assistance by packing, sorting, and distributing meals across the Region.
