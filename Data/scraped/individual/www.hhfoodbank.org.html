Our Mission

Hoosier Hills Food Bank collects, stores and distributes food to non-profit agencies that feed the hungry in Brown, Lawrence, Orange, Owen, Martin and Monroe counties in Indiana. HHFB is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization.

Over 100 non-profit member agencies serve more than 25,800 individuals each year. In 2017, we distributed 4,223,459 total pounds of food and 1,154,936 pounds of fresh produce. Since opening in 1982, HHFB has distributed over 60 million pounds of food.

HHFB is proud to be a member agency of United Way of Monroe County, an affiliate of Feeding America (formerly America’s Second Harvest), and a member of Feeding Indiana’s Hungry, Indiana’s state food bank association. Hoosier Hills Food Bank is an equal opportunity provider and employer.

How to reach us

In Bloomington:

From the EAST: Take 17th Street to Industrial Park Drive, turn left after the I69/SR37 overpass.

From the WEST: Take Vernal Pike to Industrial Park Drive, turn right before the I69/SR37 overpass.

From SR 37/I69:

Exit at either State Road 46 bypass and head west to Curry Pike, then to south to Vernal Pike OR at East Third Street and head west to Curry Pike and then north to Vernal Pike.

East Third Street is currently under construction and experiencing significant traffic delays.
