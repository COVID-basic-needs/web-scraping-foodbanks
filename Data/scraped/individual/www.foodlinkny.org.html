ROC MEAL SITES

Foodlink has partnered with the Rochester City School District to ensure children are nourished despite schools being closed due to COVID-19. Visit our blog to find out where meals are accessible for school-age children.

VOLUNTEERING

As Foodlink mobilizes a response to the COVID-19 (coronavirus) public health crisis, we have opened up select volunteer opportunities for healthy and willing members of the community who want to lend a hand. Visit the United Way’s volunteer portal to find out more.
