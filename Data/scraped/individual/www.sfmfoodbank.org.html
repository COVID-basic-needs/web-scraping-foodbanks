COVID-19 Update

The Food Bank continues to do the day-in and day-out job of making sure vulnerable neighbors get the food they need. Now, we are putting solutions in place, one by one, to be able to continue providing food and helping our community prepare for whatever challenge the COVID-19 presents.

Learn more

Together, we can end hunger in San Francisco and Marin.

Food makes it possible for kids to learn, for adults to focus better at work, and for seniors to stay healthy and active. With your support, we’re providing Food For All.

We’re expanding and upgrading our facilities to serve even more neighbors in need. Learn more:
