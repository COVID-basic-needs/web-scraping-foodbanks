Online Application

Thank you for your interest in applying to the Resource Connection’s WIC Program. WIC is a special supplemental nutrition program for Women, Infants and Children. Start your online application now by completing the information on the following page. Someone from WIC will contact you shortly.

Please note that completing the application does not guarantee your enrollment in the program. Your eligibility will be determined at your first WIC appointment.
