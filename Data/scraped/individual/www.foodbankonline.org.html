How We Do It
			





Collect Donations



Donations of food, funds and time come from individuals, businesses, foundations, USDA and other organizations




		


			





Foodbank Warehouse



The Foodbank collects, organizes, and distributes food for Partner Agencies and direct distribution programs




		


			





Partner Agencies



Agencies include food pantries, shelters, soup kitchens, mobile pantries and child nutrition programs




		


			



People Who Need Food



The food is then provided to people struggling with hunger in Southeastern Virginia and on the Eastern Shore
