FOOD BANK OF THE SOUTHERN TIER COVID-19 NEWS

The Food Bank of the Southern Tier is carefully monitoring developments regarding the spread of COVID-19 (coronavirus). A Coronavirus Contingency Planning Task Force, led by the Feeding America Disaster Services team and leaders from member food banks across the nation, is actively supporting regional food banks as we all prepare to continue our operations and distribute food to the people and communities we serve.

The Food Bank is actively consulting with Feeding America, FEMA , local VOAD (Voluntary Organizations Active in Disaster), and the USDA. Our network is leveraging other partners who are in a position to support measures to ensure access to food for the most vulnerable members of our communities during this situation. We are reaching out to the school districts and agencies we serve to determine how we can work together to meet the need.

We are continuing to serve our communities with a heightened awareness of increased need and disease prevention. Our organization has always implemented strict food safety measures. The safety of our staff, volunteers and clients is of utmost importance and we are including additional steps for extra sanitation to keep our facility, food and people as safe as possible during this potential threat.
