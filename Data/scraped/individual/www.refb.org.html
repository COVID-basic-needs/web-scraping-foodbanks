Food Connections

The REFB's Food Connections Market offers a dignified grocery shopping experience and excellent customer service. This unique program helps bridge food assistance and self-sufficiency.

Next door at Food Connections Resources, our team helps individuals and families navigate the CalFresh application process, provides free emergency food boxes, and offers information about hunger-relief programs. Anyone in need can contact the Food Connections office at (707) 523-7903.
