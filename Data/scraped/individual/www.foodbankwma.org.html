Feeding Our Neighbors in Need and Leading the Community to End Hunger

Since 1982, The Food Bank of Western Massachusetts has been feeding our neighbors in need and leading the community to end hunger. We distribute food to our member agencies in Berkshire, Franklin, Hampden and Hampshire counties.

What’s Happening?

  • Legal Food Frenzy

    March 30 – May 8, 2020
    Participating western MA Bar Associations & DA's Offices

    The Legal Food Frenzy (LFF) is a fund collection competition to support The Food Bank. Bar Associations, District Attorney’s Offices, and other legal organizations are invited to compete to win the coveted Legal Food Frenzy Cup!

    Learn More

  • Lip Sync Battle Against Hunger

    Wednesday, June 17, 2020
    Log Cabin, Holyoke, MA

    An epic Lip Sync competition where local stars ‘sync their hearts out’ in front of a live audience to raise funds for The Food Bank.

    Learn More

  • Will Bike 4 Food

    Sunday, September 27, 2020
    Lions Club Pavilion, Hatfield MA

    Will Bike 4 Food (WB4F) is an annual fun and active cycling event that allows riders of all levels to join in the fight against hunger.
