Welcome to Food Bank RGV

Today, the Food Bank has grown to become the 40th largest in the nation and 5th largest in Texas based on distribution. We are proudly affiliated with Feeding America and Feeding Texas. The Food Bank serves Hidalgo, Willacy and Cameron Counties.

OUR MISSION

The Food Bank of the Rio Grande Valley is passionately committed to improving lives through food assistance, nutrition education, and access to community services.
