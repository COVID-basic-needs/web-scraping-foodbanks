FIND Your Pantry

  • Mobile Programs

Mobile distribution allows us to deliver fresh and nutritious food directly to clients living in areas with limited access to fresh foods like produce, meat and dairy.
LEARN MORE

  • Children

The Summer Family Food Program  will provide more than 120,000 meals to children and families at 21 schools and community sites in Missouri and Illinois.
LEARN MORE

  • SNAP

For every meal the Foodbank provides, the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP) provides twelve. We recognize how vital this program is to our food-insecure neighbors, so we coordinate and assist with SNAP applications in all 26 counties we serve.
Get Help

  • Seniors

Commodity Supplemental Food Program (CSFP) boxes contain nutrient-rich foods to supplement the diets of low-income seniors. This program prevents many area seniors from having to choose between paying for food or other necessities, such as medicine or utilities.
Learn More

  • Transitional Housing

We make the transition from a shelter to a home easier by providing families with a month’s supply of food and household items.
Learn More

  • Volunteers

Each year, our volunteers donate nearly 44,000 hours of their time and energy to fighting hunger. Through a variety of projects in our Volunteer Center, they keep food moving out of our warehouse and out into the community.

  • Donate

Community support makes what we do possible. Last year, your contributions helped provide over 35 million meals to people in our region facing hunger.

  • Advocate

Hunger is a complicated problem, but we can make significant strides towards long-term solutions by addressing it in the classroom and the Capitol as well as at the dinner table. Make a difference by engaging lawmakers and other leaders in the fight against hunger.
stand up to end hunger

  • Host A Food Drive

Help stock the shelves of a local hunger relief organization by hosting a food drive at your school, office, or organization. From non-perishable food to personal care items, every donation has a real impact for someone in our community in need.
