What We Do

We provide nutritious food
to the people who need it.

Read more

1-5-homepage-2018.png

1 in 5

More than 1 in every 5 Georgia kids live in food insecure household.
Feeding America, Map the Meal Gap Study April, 2018 - Data collected in 2016.

755-homepage-2018.png

755,400

Each year, an estimated 755,400 (or 1 in 7) people in metro Atlanta and north Georgia turn to Food Bank partner agencies for food. Clients visit agencies an average of 8 times a year.
Feeding America, Hunger in America 2014 — data collected in 2013

95-homepage-2018.png

95%

95 cents of every dollar donated to ACFB (including the value of all donated food) goes directly to services in the community to help fight hunger.
