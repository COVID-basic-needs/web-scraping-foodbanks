WHAT’S IN YOUR CUPBOARD?

Are you having trouble putting together meals with what’s in your cupboard?

If you own a cell phone or computer:

 Ask Google “Recipes using….(whatever is in your cupboard or refrigerator).”

Example: “Recipes using crackers, pasta, onions and cheese.”

Up pops several recipes!
