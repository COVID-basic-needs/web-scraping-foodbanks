Our Mission

The goal of Selma Area Food Bank is to provide awareness to the poverty stricken; as well as food, support, and other necessities to the Blackbelt region of Alabama.

Due to COVID-19 we are asking that ALL agencies call to pre-order their order for pick up the day before their set appointment date. Until further notice agencies WILL NOT be allowed to come in and shop in our building. Our service hours will also only be Monday and Tuesday from 8:00am - 3:00pm until further notice.
