Service Area

The South Texas Food Bank is a 501 (c) 3 non profit organization headquartered in Laredo, Webb County, Texas. 
We service eight counties in South Texas: Val Verde, Kinney, Maverick, Dimmit, Webb, Zapata, Jim Hogg, and Starr. 
â
âWe are strategically situated about midway between these counties that stretch 300 miles- 180 northwest and 100 miles southeast along the Rio Grande River. 
â
With poverty rates ranging from 21% to a staggering 36%, the South Texas Food Bank is committed to providing help to communities who face food insecurity. â
