Hunger happens to everyday people, every day.

Look around you. There’s a good chance someone you see each day is hungry. In Tampa Bay, hundreds of thousands of people are food insecure, meaning they lack consistent and easy access to safe, nutritionally adequate food. And the problem isn’t limited only to the homeless. Many who struggle to find adequate food are working adults, children and the elderly.
