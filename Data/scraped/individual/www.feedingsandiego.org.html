GIVE TIME

GIVE FUNDS

GIVE VOICE

In order to achieve a hunger-free and healthy community, Feeding San Diego advocates for the rights of the food insecure on a federal, state, and local level. more

Learn More

ENDING HUNGER THROUGH FOOD RESCUE

Feeding San Diego is a mission to connect every person facing hunger with nutritious meals by maximizing food rescue. Through our decentralized food recovery and distribution model, every dollar donated helps provide four meals to people in need.
Learn More

Feeding Kids

1 in 6 children in San Diego struggle with hunger. We serve children, youth, and their families through 40 school pantries, 13 program sites, and 54 after school and summer meal sites. more

Feeding Families

We provide healthy meals to families across the county through 162 partner agencies, 16 mobile pantries, and 11 college distribution sites. more

Feeding Heroes

The Feeding Heroes initiative reaches every corner of the county — on or near base, in military housing areas, and in public schools serving a high percentage of military families. We serve military families and veterans through 9 program sites and multiple community partner distributions. more

Feeding Seniors

Often forced to choose between paying for food or medication, seniors struggling with hunger access fresh produce and staple items through Feeding San Diego. We provide nutritious food to seniors through 3 program sites, multiple community partner distributions, and a meal-delivery partnership with Meals on Wheels. more
