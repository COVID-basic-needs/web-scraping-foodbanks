Welcome To Norwescap


Norwescap is dedicated to creating opportunities and changing lives for thousands of low-income individuals and families in Northwest New Jersey.


Norwescap Programs Events Get Involved COVID-19 Help

Creating Opportunities. Changing Lives.


Welcome

Norwescap is a private, nonprofit corporation, established in 1965, serving the low-income population in Northwest NJ. We offer a comprehensive array of strategies, programs, and services to create opportunities and change lives.
Learn More

Get Involved

Over the years, our impact has grown, and currently, we serve over 30,000 people annually. Get involved in creating opportunities and changing lives. Join good people, doing great things for your community.

How To Help

Get Assistance

If you need help, we are here. Norwescap serves low-income individuals and families in Hunterdon, Morris, Somerset, Sussex, and Warren Counties. Learn more about how our programs can help you and your family.

Our Programs

Who We Help

A recently separated mother of two young girls came to the Career & Life Transitions Center. Though struggling financially, she was ineligible for food stamps. Without the extra cushion provided by such benefits as Child Support and Rental Assistance, she lived by borrowing against her credit. She has a business degree and decided that she needed a career change and further schooling because she could not afford the commute back and forth to New York City for her job as a single parent.
